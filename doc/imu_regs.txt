0x02
SIGNAL_PATH_RESET
Triggers a software reset of the sensor during initialization

0x1F
PWR_MGMT0
Enables the sensors by switching the gyroscope and accelerometer into Low Noise (LN) mode

0x20
GYRO_CONFIG0
Sets the gyroscope's full-scale range (e.g., ±250 dps) and its output data rate (e.g., 100 Hz)

0x21
ACCEL_CONFIG0
Sets the accelerometer's full-scale range (e.g., ±2g) and its output data rate (e.g., 100 Hz)

0x0B
ACCEL_DATA_X1
The starting point for reading 12 bytes of raw motion data, covering all X, Y, and Z axes for both accelerometer and gyroscope
